Projects

  • 2021.01 - Present
    Transparent Ultrasound Transducer Based Devices for Ultrasound Neuromodulation and Imaging
    Transparent ultrasound transducer (TUT) platform for multimodal imaging and ultrasound neuromodulation, in vitro and in vivo.
    • Demonstrated feasibility of TUT implantation for awake mouse optical & photoacoustic imaging
    • Examining ultrasound effects on neuronal and non-neuronal cells across stimulation parameters
  • 2022.01 - Present
    Multi-Parametric Ultrafast Ultrasound and Photoacoustic Imaging Platform for Mice Brain Imaging
    Combined ultrafast ultrasound and photoacoustic system to assess cerebrovascular and neural changes in rodent brains.
    • Super-resolution ultrasound methods, code development, and data analysis
    • Toward applications in neurological disease models
  • 2021.01 - Present
    Development of Transparent Ultrasound Transducer
    Development and characterization of PMN-PT and lithium niobate-based transparent ultrasound transducers.
    • Compared PMN-PT vs. lithium niobate performance and noise
    • Assessed PMN-PT as a viable alternative for multimodal optical & photoacoustic setups
  • 2016.01 - 2020.01
    Fiber-Optic Interferometer Hydrophone Sensor Array
    Design of sensor arrays, homodyne PGC interrogation, passive path imbalance measurement, and TDM multiplexing.
    • Homodyne phase-generated carrier implementation and acoustic signal recovery
    • Five-sensor interferometer array with time-division multiplexing
  • 2016.01 - 2017.12
    Chaos in Erbium-Doped Fiber Ring Lasers
    Wide-bandwidth chaos demonstration and analysis via Rosenstein's algorithm.
  • 2014.01 - 2015.12
    Digital Communication Using Chaotic Signals
    MATLAB implementation and evaluation of chaotic modulation/demodulation schemes and jamming performance.
  • 2012.01 - 2015.12
    NSIT Solar Car Concept
    Solar electric vehicle design and integration of battery pack, solar panels, and DC motor.
